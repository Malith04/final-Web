About RAJANS ICT

Information and Communication Technology Society of Dharmaraja has an eventful history of more than a decade. We have conducted a range of extracurricular activities, educational programs, and technology-centered events to enhance the knowledge of ICT among our colleagues. It’s our ambition to empower our colleagues with the right blend of technology and wisdom.

  • Presentation

RULES & REGULATIONS

  • This competition is open for students who are in grades 6-9.
  • Participants can use any presentation software they prefer. (Ex- PowerPoint, libre office, Open office…)
  • A participant can submit only one project in this competition.
  • A presentation should be present to one of the following topics.
    • Computer history
    • Internet
    • Artificial Intelligence
    • Social Media
    • Future of ICT
  • Presentation must contain 10-15 slides.
  • You may submit the project by uploading the project into a CLOUD STORAGE and providing us the LINK.
  • RAJANS students can submit the project physically (by storing in a PEN DRIVE or CD/DVD)
  • Project must be submitted before 11th June, 2023.
  • A group of professionals will judge and rank the projects.
  • Prices and certificates will be issued to first, second and third places.
  • Graphic Designing

RULES & REGULATIONS

  • This competition is open for students who are in grades 6-7, 8-9, 10-11 and 12-13.
  • One competitor can submit only one project.
  • The design should be of A4 size.
  • Competitors are allowed to use any graphic design software they prefer. (Ex-Photoshop, Adobe illustrator etc.)
  • The design must be according to the given topics.
    • An invitation, poster, notice for the ICT Day
    • Souvenir Book Cover for ICT Day
    • Flyers/ handbills about an ICT competition 
  • Design must be submitted in JPG, PNG format.
  • You may submit the project by uploading the project into a CLOUD STORAGE and providing us the LINK.
  • RAJANS students can submit the project physically (by storing in a PEN DRIVE or CD/DVD)
  • The top 5 posters will be shared on the Official Instagram and Facebook page of the ICT Society.
  • The competitors MUST have full copyrights of their submitted work. (The Drawing must be signed by the artist)
  • Project must be submitted before 11th June, 2023.
  • A group of professionals will judge and rank the projects.
  • Prices and certificates will be issued to first, second and third places.

RULES & REGULATIONS

For Inter-School Students

  • A team must consist of 5 members including the leader.
  • A team can consist of players’ of various grades and classes.
  • The video game “Valorant” will be played in the competition.
  • An approval letter from parent / guardian must be provided.
  • Organizers will not take any responsibility for participants technical failures.
  • The In Game Names (IGN)/ Riot IDs of the team members must be provided.
  • All 5 players must be online at least 30 minutes before the tournament begin.
  • The google form with the required details must be submitted before 31st May, 2023.
  • Prices and certificates will be issued to the winning team.

For RAJANS only

  • A team must consist of 5 members including the leader.
  • A team can consist of players’ of various grades and classes.
  • The video game “Call Of Duty 4” will be played in the competition.
  • The matches will be played physically at the Science Section ICT LAB
  • The google form with the required details must be submitted before 31st May, 2023.
  • Prices and certificates will be issued to the winning team.
  • Quiz

RULES & REGULATIONS

  • FOR RAJANS 
    • The competition is open for all the students from grade 6 to 12.
    • Each class from each grade can enter the competition.
    • A team must consist of four members.
    • Classes in the same grade will compete.
    • Replacement of any participant of a team is not allowed after registration.
    • The key subject area for the competition will be related to the genaral ICT knowledge of the students.
    • The quiz will be held physically, and it will consist of 25 Multiple Choice questions.
    • The final round will be held as a physical event at Dharmaraja College Auditorium.
  • FOR ALL ISLAND STUDENTS
    • The Competition is Open for grade 11, 12, 13 students.
    • Two teams are allowed to enter a school and each team should consist of four members.
    • Replacement of any participant of a team is not allowed after registration.
    • The key subject area for the competition will be related to the ICT knowledge of the students. Questions will be asked from the following key areas,
      • History
      • GIT
      • Programming (Python) 
      • Networking
      • IOT
      • Software Developing
    • The top 10 schools will be selected for the Semi-Final round.
    • The teacher in charge of the school’s representative association will be informed if the team is selected for the Semi-Final round.
    • The final round will be held as a physical event at Dharmaraja College Auditorium.
