Security & Privacy Policy

Last Updated:

1. Data Protection

At PowerFit Gym, we take your data security seriously with these measures:

  • Military-Grade Encryption: All member data protected with 256-bit SSL/TLS encryption
  • Secure Payment Processing: Partnered with Stripe and PayPal for fraud-protected transactions
  • Biometric Access: Staff access to systems requires multi-factor authentication
  • Regular Audits: Quarterly penetration testing by cybersecurity experts

2. Privacy Compliance

We exceed industry standards for member privacy:

  • GDPR Certified: European member data never leaves compliant servers
  • CCPA Ready: California members can opt-out of data sharing with one click
  • HIPAA Compliant: Medical waiver data stored with healthcare-grade security

Your Rights: Request a copy of your data, corrections, or full deletion anytime through your member portal.

3. Account Security

Help us keep your PowerFit account secure:

  • Password Requirements: Minimum 12 characters with mixed case, numbers, and symbols (e.g., FitLife@2024!)
  • Session Timeout: Automatic logout after 30 minutes of inactivity
  • Device Management: View and revoke connected devices in your profile
  • Login Alerts: Instant email/SMS notifications for new logins

Never share your credentials - our staff will never ask for your password.
